## Adapted Method: taste-skill

`dials.md` and `hands.md` adapt the **dials mechanism** (DESIGN_VARIANCE, MOTION_INTENSITY,
VISUAL_DENSITY), **layout-pattern vocabulary** (bento / split-screen / editorial /
sidebar+canvas / Z-F-pattern / asymmetric-hero), and **aesthetic hands** (minimalist / soft)
from:

- **Repository:** [leonxlnx/taste-skill](https://github.com/Leonxlnx/taste-skill)
- **License:** MIT, © 2026 Leonxlnx
- **What was adapted:** the design-decision method (dials, layout vocabulary, hands).
  Palette, fonts, GSAP references, and hard rules already present in
  `emil-design` and `redesign` (and the project's own design-system tokens, when one
  exists) were NOT re-imported to avoid duplication.
- **What was NOT copied:** taste-skill's `SKILL.md` verbatim text, their color palettes
  (white/pastel), their typeface defaults (Inter/Helvetica), or any GSAP-specific guidance.

## Optional Inspiration Source: awesome-design-md

[VoltAgent/awesome-design-md](https://github.com/VoltAgent/awesome-design-md) — a collection
of 72 brand `DESIGN.md` files. Referenced as a WebFetch-on-demand source for greenfield
custom hands; no files vendored in this repository.
